# ThoughtNest
Therapy sessions usually last about one hour per week, leaving 167 hours where individuals are on their own. During that time, many important moments can occur — emotional breakthroughs, difficult setbacks, stressful situations, or personal reflections. Unfortunately, by the time the next therapy session arrives, people often struggle to remember or clearly explain what happened.  This application bridges that gap by allowing users to capture their thoughts, emotions, and experiences in real time.
